\# Source Notes



\## Skyscanner



Primary role: flight discovery and flight pricing.



Expected fields:



\- Origin airport

\- Destination airport

\- Departure date

\- Return date

\- Airline

\- Stops

\- Duration

\- Price

\- Booking/provider details



\## Expedia



Primary role: lodging search, hotel pricing, and possible flight/hotel comparison data.



Expected fields:



\- Hotel name

\- Destination

\- Check-in date

\- Check-out date

\- Total price

\- Nightly price

\- Guest rating

\- Amenities

\- Property type



\## Tripadvisor



Primary role: hotel quality, review signals, location quality, and lodging comparison.



Expected fields:



\- Hotel name

\- Bubble rating

\- Review count

\- Traveler ranking

\- Location/review quality

\- Amenities

\- Price where available



\## Viator



Primary role: tours, attractions, experiences, and activity cost estimates.



Expected fields:



\- Experience name

\- Destination

\- Available dates

\- Price

\- Rating

\- Review count

\- Duration

\- Category



\## Travel Advisory



Primary role: safety and risk screening.



Expected fields:



\- Country

\- Advisory level

\- Summary

\- Key risks

\- Safety recommendations

\- Traveler impact where available
